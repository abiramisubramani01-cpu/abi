Famous Tourist Spots in Tamil Nadu

Ooty

Ooty is a beautiful hill station in the Nilgiri Hills, famous for tea plantations, gardens, lakes, and cool weather.

Kodaikanal

Kodaikanal is known as the "Princess of Hill Stations" and is famous for Kodai Lake, Coaker's Walk, and scenic valleys.

Mahabalipuram

Mahabalipuram is a UNESCO World Heritage Site known for ancient rock-cut temples, Shore Temple, and stone sculptures.

Madurai

Madurai is one of India's oldest cities and is famous for the Meenakshi Amman Temple and rich cultural heritage.

Rameswaram

Rameswaram is a sacred pilgrimage destination known for Ramanathaswamy Temple, beaches, and Pamban Bridge.

Kanyakumari

Kanyakumari is the southernmost tip of India, famous for sunrise, sunset, Vivekananda Rock Memorial, and Thiruvalluvar Statue.
